# Vitto Website — Sitemap & Page Strategy

---

## Overview

The Vitto website is positioned for a specific audience: C-suite and senior technology/risk leaders at Banks, NBFCs, and MFIs. These are people who evaluate infrastructure vendors by reading documentation, not by watching demo videos. Every page is designed around what a CTO or CRO needs to see to feel confident enough to request a technical demo.

---

## Page 1 — Homepage (`/`)

**Strategic reason:** First impression and navigation hub. Must answer three questions in the first screen: What is Vitto? Is it relevant to us? What do we do next?

**Primary audience:** First-time visitors — CTOs, CROs, Digital Transformation Heads. Will likely arrive via referral, LinkedIn, or a conference mention.

**Conversion goal:** "Book a Demo" (primary) | "Explore Platform" (secondary)

**Key sections:**
- Hero: AI-native positioning, not retrofitted
- Problem: Four problems they recognise from their own institution
- Solution: Why Vitto's approach is structurally different
- AI Modules: Six modules — signals depth of product
- Business Impact: Quantified outcomes
- Social proof: Partner logos + testimonials
- CTA banner

---

## Page 2 — AI-First Platform Page (`/platform`)

**Strategic reason:** Converts sceptics. When a CTO asks "but how does the AI actually work?", this page must answer with real technical depth. This is the page a senior engineer will read before agreeing to a demo.

**Primary audience:** CTO, Chief Architecture Officer, Head of Technology. Technical evaluators with the ability to identify shallow AI positioning.

**Conversion goal:** Book a Technical Demo | Read API Docs

**Content structure:**
- Six module deep-dives (accordion)
- Agentic AI section with SLM vs public LLM comparison
- Architecture diagram (RAG pipeline)
- Integration and deployment model

---

## Page 3 — Full Stack Automation (`/automation`)

**Strategic reason:** Demonstrates operational breadth. Operations and product leads need to see that Vitto covers the workflows their team runs today — not just the "AI" parts. This page builds confidence that Vitto can replace multiple point solutions.

**Primary audience:** Head of Credit Operations, COO, Product/Technology Leads. People evaluating whether Vitto can replace their current LOS/LMS/CRM stack.

**Conversion goal:** Book a Demo | Download Feature Checklist

**Content structure:**
- Five operational layers with expandable module detail
- Module count and workflow coverage
- Positioning statement: "Transaction system vs. decisioning system"

---

## Page 4 — Collections Intelligence (`/collections`)

**Strategic reason:** Collections is often the first entry point for conversations with NBFCs and MFIs where portfolio quality is under pressure. A dedicated page for a specific persona-driven problem signals that Vitto understands their context.

**Primary audience:** Head of Collections, CRO, CEO of smaller NBFCs/MFIs with active NPA pressure.

**Conversion goal:** Book a Collections-Specific Demo

**Content:**
- Propensity-to-pay model explanation
- Channel optimisation and allotment workflow
- Recovery improvement claims with methodology
- Field agent app preview

---

## Page 5 — Agentic AI (`/agentic`)

**Strategic reason:** Agentic AI is the most differentiated and most misunderstood capability. It requires its own page to explain the SLM approach, the RAG architecture, and why generic LLMs cannot serve this use case in a regulated environment.

**Primary audience:** CTOs, Digital Innovation leads. People who have already experimented with ChatGPT internally and want to understand what a compliant alternative looks like.

**Conversion goal:** Schedule a Technical Architecture Review

---

## Page 6 — API Infrastructure (`/api`)

**Strategic reason:** Fintech partners, co-lending institutions, and technically sophisticated banks want to embed Vitto capabilities via API. A dedicated API page signals that Vitto is a platform, not just a product.

**Primary audience:** Architects, developers, fintech partnership leads.

**Conversion goal:** Access API Docs | Apply for Sandbox Access

**Content:**
- REST API overview
- Authentication model
- Webhook infrastructure
- Sandbox environment

---

## Page 7 — About / Why Vitto (`/about`)

**Strategic reason:** Institutional buyers want to understand who is building this and why. The founding story, the team's BFSI background, and the company's design philosophy establish credibility beyond the product.

**Primary audience:** CEOs, MDs, and senior leadership evaluating a long-term infrastructure partner. Also relevant for due diligence by investment teams.

**Conversion goal:** No hard CTA — awareness and trust-building page

**Content:**
- Founding story and thesis
- Team backgrounds (BFSI and engineering)
- Design philosophy: why AI-native vs. retrofitted
- Advisors and investors

---

## Page 8 — Contact / Request Demo (`/contact`)

**Strategic reason:** Conversion endpoint. Must make it easy for a warm lead to schedule a meeting without friction. Every page on the site drives here.

**Primary audience:** Any qualified visitor who has decided to engage.

**Conversion goal:** Form submission → scheduled demo call

**Content:**
- Demo request form with institution type and primary use case
- Calendar embed (Calendly or similar)
- What to expect from the demo session
- Contact email and WhatsApp for direct reach

---

## Page 9 — Self Sign-Up (`/signup`)

**Strategic reason:** Self-service entry point for institutions that want to explore before speaking to sales. Captures qualified leads with a lower-friction commitment than a demo request.

**Primary audience:** Technology or product leads who prefer to evaluate on their own timeline.

**Conversion goal:** OTP-verified sign-up → lead created in CRM

**Flow:**
1. Email or mobile OTP verification
2. Institution details form
3. Confirmation screen → team follow-up within 24 hours

---

## Navigation Architecture

```
Homepage
├── Platform (AI Modules deep-dive)
├── Automation (Full stack operational modules)
├── Collections (Dedicated vertical page)
├── Agentic AI (SLM + RAG architecture)
├── API (Developer / partner page)
├── About
└── Contact / Demo
    └── Sign Up (self-service)
```

**Persistent CTAs:** "Book a Demo" in the navbar (right-aligned) on every page.
